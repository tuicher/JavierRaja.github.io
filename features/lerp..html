Introduction

Too many times on your life, even more if you are a computer graphics' / videogames' programmer, you end using a Linear interpolation (Usually named LERP). In this short code, you can generate images with N x M resolution defining 4 color. Thoose color will be positioned on the 4 corner of the image, then the algorithm will fill the rest of the image with a pretty color fade between them.
